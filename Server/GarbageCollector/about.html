Welcome

Proposed system, Garbage collecting Robot will help to collect garbage in an indoor environment (ex: hostel) effectively . In the modern society , positive impact of robotic works contribute an immense service to the human beings in technical, agricultural ,industrial and social life improvement. Taking the maximum advantage of robotic actions, the proposed system will implement a garbage collecting robot. Nowadays Garbage is a main problem in worldwide attention. Improper disposal of waste matters, shows a high possibility of spreading communicable diseases and environmental pollution. Lacking of proper waste management is mostly noticed in indoor environments. Low budget allocation towards indoor environment cleanliness, directly affects for the above mentioned problem. Hence to overcome these obstacles and difficulties in manual existing system new system will be designed.

Objective

To introduce effective garbage collecting robot in an indoor mapped area with the intention of minimizing difficulties in existing manual system. Moreover, reduction of manual collecting labor and job hazards related to garbage collecting. Avoid environmental pollution and reduce carbon footprints on the earth.

  • Conduct feasibility study to identify the drawbacks in manual existing system.
  • Identification of available solutions to collect garbage effectively.
  • Recognition of most effective and sustainable path to substitute the manual system by a robot.
  • Development of a cost effective robot, to do the garbage collecting process effectively.
  • Reduction of spreading communicable diseases and environmental pollution.
  • eduction of job hazards that can be occur during the manual collecting process.
